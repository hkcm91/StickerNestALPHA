## GENERAL POSTING NOTES

1. **Every post should be understandable by someone who has never heard of StickerNest.** If it requires context from a previous post, it's too deep.

2. **Lead with the concept, not features.** "Infinite canvas where stickers are icons and widgets are apps" is the one-liner. Everything branches from there.

3. **The personal story is a strength.** Solo self-taught dev building between retail shifts — that's compelling. Use it.

4. **Show the product.** Every post should ideally have a visual — screen recording, screenshot, or demo. The brand voice doc says "show don't tell" and that's exactly right here.

5. **Don't explain the architecture.** Nobody outside dev Twitter cares about 7-layer architecture or typed event buses. Save that for dev-specific content later, once people know what the product IS.

6. **Suggested first week cadence:**
   - Twitter: Post Tweet 1 or Tweet 5 first (concept intro). Follow up 2-3 days later with Tweet 2 (personal story).
   - TikTok: Post TikTok 1 or TikTok 3 first (product demo). Follow up with TikTok 2 (personal story).
