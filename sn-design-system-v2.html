Design Principles

The rules behind the system

01 — Breathe
Nothing is static. Ambient background orbs drift, surfaces pulse, active states glow. The canvas is alive — but never distracting. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
02 — Respond
Motion is adaptive. Subtle at rest, intensifies on hover, springs back on release. Every element telegraphs interactivity through physics, not decoration.
03 — Refract
Surfaces are translucent, not opaque. Backdrop blur creates depth hierarchy. The hex pattern and grain texture bleed through glass — the canvas is always present.
04 — Warm
Deep charcoals, not pure black. Warm coral accents, not cold blue. This is a space you want to inhabit — Her aesthetic, not hospital lighting. Never "cute autumn AI."
05 — Chrome
Y2K futurism: holographic borders, chrome text gradients, metallic shimmers. But surgical — reserved for emphasis, never wallpaper. The accent, not the palette.
06 — Accessible
WCAG AA minimum. prefers-reduced-motion kills all animation. Focus-visible rings on every interactive. High-contrast theme as escape hatch. Beauty serves function.

Anti-Patterns

What this design system actively rejects

✕ Autumn AI Aesthetic
No pumpkin oranges, leaf browns, cozy cabin vibes. That entire palette is a AI cliche now.
✕ Pure Black (#000)
Deep charcoal with warmth. #0C0C10 not #000000. Pure black flattens depth and kills glass effects.
✕ Over-Rounded Corners
14px is our standard. No pill shapes on cards. No border-radius: 9999px on containers. Precision, not softness.
✕ Pastel Everything
Accent colors have saturation and presence. Muted pastels read as timid. Coral is warm, not washed out.
✕ Gradient Blob Backgrounds
Our ambient orbs are subtle (0.15 opacity) and move slowly. No homepage hero gradient meshes at full saturation.
✕ Gratuitous Animation
Every motion has purpose. No bounce effects on load. No spinning logos. No parallax scrolling on landing pages.
