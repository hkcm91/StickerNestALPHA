## Hero Copy

**Short (for headers and ads):**
Build interactive worlds on an infinite canvas. Place stickers, run widgets, wire them together — and share it all in real time.

**Medium (for landing page hero):**
StickerNest is a spatial platform where you build interactive spaces on an infinite canvas. Decorate with stickers, add interactive widgets, connect them with visual pipelines, and collaborate with anyone in real time. Your canvas is your desktop — stickers are icons, widgets are apps, and everything just works.

**Long (for about page):**
StickerNest reimagines how people create and share interactive digital spaces. Instead of static pages or rigid grids, you get an infinite canvas — a boundless surface where stickers, widgets, and data flow together. Place a sticky note next to a live timer next to a data visualization, wire them together with visual pipelines, and invite your team to build alongside you with live cursors and real-time sync. Whether you're prototyping a dashboard, building a game, designing a mood board, or creating an interactive presentation, StickerNest gives you the spatial freedom to work the way your brain actually thinks.

## Value Propositions

**For creators:**
Build and publish interactive widgets that thousands of people can install and use. The Widget Lab gives you a full IDE in your browser — write, test, and ship without leaving StickerNest.

**For teams:**
Collaborate in real time on a shared canvas. See each other's cursors, co-edit documents, and build together. Roles and permissions keep everything secure.

**For developers:**
A typed SDK, event bus architecture, and sandboxed iframe runtime. Build widgets with standard HTML/JS/CSS — no proprietary framework to learn. Publish to the Marketplace and reach every StickerNest user.

**For communities:**
Create shared public spaces — game tables, community boards, interactive galleries — that anyone can visit and interact with. Publish your canvas with a custom URL and share it with the world.
