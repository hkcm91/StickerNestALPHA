# Tier Comparison

Pricing page copy and feature matrix for StickerNest plans.

## Plan Overview Copy

### Free
**Get started with the essentials.**
Create canvases, install widgets from the Marketplace, collaborate with others, and write canvas scripts. Everything you need to start building interactive spaces.

### Creator — $X/month
**Build and publish your own widgets.**
Everything in Free, plus the full Widget Lab IDE — code editor, live preview, AI generation, and the publish pipeline. Build widgets with standard HTML/JS/CSS and ship them to the Marketplace.

### Pro — $X/month
**For power users and serious creators.**
Everything in Creator, plus higher limits on storage, canvases, and collaborators. Advanced analytics on your published widgets, priority support, and early access to new features.

### Enterprise — Custom pricing
**For teams and organizations.**
Everything in Pro, plus workspace administration, SSO/SAML, custom branding, dedicated account management, SLA guarantees, and volume licensing.
