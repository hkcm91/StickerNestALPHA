# Account and Billing

## Creating an Account

Sign up at stickernest.com with your email or use one of the social login options: Google, GitHub, or Discord. You can link multiple login methods to the same account from your settings page.

## Plans and Tiers

StickerNest offers four account tiers:

**Free** — get started with the basics. Create canvases, install widgets from the Marketplace, collaborate with others. You can write canvas scripts (lightweight automation) but cannot access the full Widget Lab.

**Creator** — everything in Free, plus access to the Widget Lab where you can build, test, and publish your own widgets. This is the tier for people who want to make things, not just use them.

**Pro** — everything in Creator, plus higher limits on storage, canvases, and collaborators. Priority support and advanced analytics on your published widgets.

**Enterprise** — custom pricing for teams and organizations. Includes workspace-level administration, SSO, custom branding, dedicated support, and SLA guarantees.

## Billing

Billing is handled through Stripe (credit/debit cards) or PayPal. You can view your current plan, update payment methods, and manage your subscription from **Settings > Billing**.

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep your current tier's features until then.

## Account Settings

From **Settings**, you can:

- Update your display name and avatar
- Change your email address
- Link or unlink social login providers (Google, GitHub, Discord)
- Change your password
- View your current plan and billing history
- Delete your account

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent. It removes your profile, all canvases you own (collaborators will lose access), all uploaded assets, all widget state, and all published widgets. Canvases shared with you by others are not affected.

You'll see a confirmation dialog with a summary of what will be deleted before the action is final.
