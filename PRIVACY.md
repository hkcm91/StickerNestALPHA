# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 27, 2026

## Introduction

StickerNest ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name and profile information you choose to provide

### Notion Integration
When you connect your Notion workspace:
- We receive an access token to read/write to databases you explicitly grant access to
- We do not access workspaces, pages, or databases you haven't shared with StickerNest
- We store your Notion workspace name and icon for display purposes

### Usage Data
- Canvas and widget configurations you create
- Interaction data to improve the service

### Payment Information
- Payment processing is handled by Stripe
- We do not store credit card numbers
- We store subscription status and Stripe customer IDs

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the StickerNest service
- To authenticate you and manage your account
- To connect to third-party services (Notion, etc.) that you authorize
- To process payments and manage subscriptions
- To communicate service updates and respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security
- API keys you provide are encrypted at rest
- We use HTTPS for all data transmission
- Access tokens for third-party services are stored encrypted

## Third-Party Services

We integrate with:
- **Supabase** — database and authentication
- **Stripe** — payment processing
- **Notion** — database integration (user-authorized)
- **AI providers** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Replicate) — only when you provide your own API keys

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Disconnect third-party integrations at any time
- Export your canvas data

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Upon account deletion, your data is removed within 30 days
- Anonymized usage analytics may be retained for service improvement

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: privacy@stickernest.app
