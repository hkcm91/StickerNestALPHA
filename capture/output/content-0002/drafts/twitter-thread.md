## Tweet 3
The refinement overlay is a glass modal that slides in before generation.

Three sections:
1. AI-generated clarifying questions (specific to YOUR prompt)
2. Compatible widgets already on your canvas
3. Quick toggles — interactive, dark mode, event emission

All of this enriches the prompt before the AI touches it.

## Tweet 4
The clarifying questions are the key.

Type "weather dashboard" and the AI asks:
- What data source? (API, manual, mock?)
- Visual style? (minimal, detailed, dark?)
- What interactions? (click forecast, drag timeline?)

Your answers get woven into the enriched prompt automatically.

## Tweet 5
The compatible widgets section is subtle but powerful.

If you already have a "Data Table" widget on your canvas, the overlay shows it. Select it and the AI generates your new widget with matching port contracts — so they can talk to each other via pipelines out of the box.
